Sectors We Serve

Industries We Serve

Industrial utility expertise deployed across eight key manufacturing, process and institutional sectors.

Pharmaceuticals

The pharmaceutical sector demands precise utility control -- process steam, purified water, HVAC and compressed air all to GMP standards.

  • IBR-compliant boilers for clean steam generation
  • High-purity water systems (WFI, purified water, RO)
  • Oil-free compressed air for process applications
  • Cooling for reactors, condensers and cold rooms
  • Water treatment chemicals compatible with GMP requirements
  • Utility validation and documentation support

Chemicals

Chemical plants operate continuous, high-intensity utilities. Reliability, corrosion control and safety compliance are critical to every system we design and maintain.

  • Process steam at high pressures and temperatures
  • Cooling water for exothermic reactions
  • Corrosion-resistant cooling tower materials
  • Chemical-compatible water treatment programmes
  • Safe chemical supply and handling guidance
  • Utility audit and energy optimisation studies

Food & Beverage

Food and beverage manufacturers need hygienic utilities -- steam for cooking and sterilisation, water for processing and cooling for preservation and packaging.

  • Food-grade steam boiler consultancy
  • Hygienic water treatment and RO systems
  • Cooling for blast chillers, breweries and dairies
  • Food-safe water treatment chemicals
  • Compressed air for packaging and conveying
  • CIP system chemical supply

Textiles

Textile processes are steam-intensive and water-heavy. We help textile manufacturers reduce utility costs while maintaining production quality.

  • Steam boilers for dyeing, finishing and humidification
  • Softened water for dyeing machines
  • Cooling towers for compressors and process cooling
  • Effluent treatment for dyehouse wastewater
  • Boiler and cooling tower chemical supply
  • Energy efficiency programmes for steam systems

Dying and Pigment Industries

Dye and pigment manufacturing involves intensive process heat, precise water quality and demanding effluent standards. Apex Utility keeps these utilities reliable, compliant and efficient.

  • Process steam boilers for reaction and drying stages
  • High-purity water treatment for dye synthesis
  • Corrosion-resistant cooling towers for reactor cooling
  • Effluent and wastewater treatment for pigment discharge
  • Chemical dosing systems for colour-stable process water
  • Compressed air supply for pigment drying and packaging

GIDC & Industrial Estates

Gujarat's GIDC industrial estates host diverse manufacturing units that need dependable shared and dedicated utility infrastructure. Apex Utility supports GIDC-based plants with consultancy, equipment supply and rapid maintenance response.

  • Utility planning for new GIDC plot setups
  • Boiler, compressor and cooling tower installation
  • Common infrastructure utility audits
  • Water treatment for estate-wide water quality
  • Chemical and material supply within GIDC zones
  • Rapid response maintenance for estate units

Hotels & Hospitality

Hotels rely on continuous hot water, climate control and clean water for guest comfort and back-of-house operations. We help hospitality properties run efficient, reliable utility systems around the clock.

  • Boilers for centralised hot water and laundry
  • HVAC and cooling tower systems for guest comfort
  • RO and water treatment for kitchens and guest use
  • Compressed air for maintenance and laundry equipment
  • Water treatment chemicals for pools and cooling systems
  • Energy audits to reduce utility operating costs

Hospitals & Healthcare

Hospitals need uninterrupted, compliant utility systems -- steam for sterilisation, controlled HVAC and treated water for patient care. Apex Utility supports healthcare facilities with reliable, standards-compliant utility solutions.

  • Steam boilers for sterilisation and CSSD departments
  • HVAC systems for critical care and OT areas
  • RO and purified water systems for medical use
  • Compressed air for medical and instrumentation use
  • Water treatment chemicals suited to healthcare compliance
  • Preventive maintenance to avoid utility downtime
