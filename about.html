Utility Services

Who We Are

Built on Engineering Expertise. Trusted by Industry.

Apex Industrial Utility Consultancy and Services was founded with a singular purpose: to bring genuine engineering expertise to industrial utility challenges that most companies treat as afterthoughts.

We operate across boilers, water treatment, cooling towers, air compressors, industrial chemicals and utility consultancy -- giving our clients a single, accountable partner for every aspect of their plant's utility infrastructure.

Based in Gujarat, we serve pharmaceutical, chemical, food & beverage, textile, engineering and manufacturing plants across India, providing consultancy, installation support, operational guidance and reliable material supply.
