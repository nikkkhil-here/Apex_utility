Boiler Engineering

Boilers

IBR & Non-IBR boiler consultancy, installation and operational support.

Overview

Expert Boilers Services

Apex Utility provides end-to-end boiler services -- from initial consultancy and IBR compliance guidance through to installation supervision, commissioning, operator training and long-term operation & maintenance support.

Our boiler engineers have hands-on experience with fire-tube, water-tube, coil-type and composite boilers across pharmaceutical, chemical, food processing and textile industries.

What We Offer

Detailed Services

IBR Boiler Consultancy

Expert guidance on Indian Boiler Regulations -- design review, documentation and liaison with boiler inspectors.

Non-IBR Boiler Services

Supply, installation and maintenance of non-IBR steam generators and hot water systems.

Boiler Installation Support

Site supervision, pipework layout, fitting and initial steam-up for new boiler installations.

Commissioning & Testing

Full commissioning procedure including safety valve testing, water treatment start-up and load trials.

Operation & Maintenance

Preventive maintenance schedules, operational training and breakdown support for in-service boilers.

Performance & Efficiency

Combustion analysis, flue gas monitoring, blowdown management and steam trap audits to cut fuel costs.

Applications
